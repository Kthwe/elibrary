Introduction  
Following the oil crisis of the 1970's energy experts began to  
explore the viability of solar energy as a substitute for petroleum-  
based fuels. This was a major step forward in the movement toward a  
sustainable energy source. Although solar-energy performance has  
mixed, and its use in industrialized countries has been minimal, recent  
developments have led to promising results in some rural areas of  
Myanmar. Minbu Solar Power Plant have proven solar energy is a  
viable source of energy for electrification increase with low load  
energy density. Interestingly enough, success was not a result of  
superior technology, but rather changes in the alternate power source.  
This case study focuses specifically on Myanmar.  
Energy in Myanmar  
Despite a new focus on renewable resources, demand for  
petroleum grew at rapid number. Most mainland electrification in  
Myanmar is received from Law Pi Ta Waterfall, which is a problem  
in Summer. But due to rising operating costs, moves were made to  
electrify rural parts of the Country by way of solar energy. One of the  
most successful experiences with solar electrification came in Minbu.

Solar Energy in Minbu  
Although early phase with solar energy was 50MWDC, the  
main benefit to installing the photovoltaic system was developing as  
an appropriate approach. With completed project, a total of 220  
MWDC can be provided and may satisfy up to 350,000,000 kWh  
annually, equivalent to power usage by 200,000 households.  
The System: Reasons for Success  
1. Good maintenance, provided by local technicians and the use of  
foreign technicians.  
2. Good location, Minbu is tropical city in Myanmar and provide  
as much sun as needed.  
3. Good timing, Minbu power plant may even support even in  
summer as it based on solar system completely overthrowing  
conventional power sources.  
Conclusion  
An increase in concern about global conservation across the  
world lead to search in alternative source apart from coal power plant  
and nuclear power plants. Solar Panel, unique invention to conserve  
the world, has becoming a solution. Even though power factor of the  
Solar Panel is low, it is possible to enhance the power plant with solar  
tracking system.
