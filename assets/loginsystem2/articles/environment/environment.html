Case Study on Environment based on nearby stream  
Introduction  
Clean and green environment; Environment is where we reside in.  
‘Clean’ in general means keeping dust and garbage free from  
somewhere. ‘Green’ does not necessary means “green”, it means the  
general color of trees. So clean and green environment means  
‘environment with trees free from garbage’. But there is a question to be  
asked, are we really keeping the environment clean? I will discuss in  
detail.  
Background history of our stream  
It existed a very long time ago. It was unclear whether it was man-  
made or natural. But one thing for sure, it was flowing for my whole life.  
It was beautiful as my mother mentioned. And fishes can be captured  
very easily. One can catch fishes as he/she put his/her toes in the sand.  
But when I grew older, it was not as she mentioned since fishes are very  
rare. Let alone capturing in the sand mentioned earlier, it was impossible  
to capture with net. So, what caused such situation.  
Today’s situation  
The little stream that I mentioned was gone two or three years ago.  
It is possible to recognize as a stream because of the sand lines. Lots of  
garbage blocking its path cause the water stop from flowing. Over  
populated near the stream causes such problem. Now, I would be  
imagining if I talk about the stream I used to swim when I was young. It  
would be a bed time story for them as it was for me. It is something that  
can’t be reclaimed from the mother nature.
